Supply Chain Vendor and SKU Tracker

A personal vendor knowledge base for supply chain professionals and independent sourcing brokers.

About This Application

This application helps users organize vendor information, product categories, personal ratings, delivery performance observations, and general sourcing notes.

Vendors can be associated with the SKUs they supply. Users can also select a SKU to view its item details and all vendors that can supply it.

How to Use

  1. Add and manage vendor information.
  2. Record vendor ratings and sourcing notes.
  3. Associate vendors with the SKUs they supply.
  4. View each SKU and its available vendors.
